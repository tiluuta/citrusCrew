Keyring uses Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) technology to make door security access easier.


RFID technology uses radio waves to identify people or objects.

It is used in badges and access cards to wirelessly unlock protected spaces that the user has access to.

A RFID card is embedded in the ring for increased accessibility.
